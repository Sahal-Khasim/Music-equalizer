What is PEAK Music-Equalizer

PEAK is an amazing, simple and user friendly music equalizer and music visualization tool. you can create your own fantastic video with visual effects by simply creating a user profile add your favuorite music enjoy with your visual effects. Trust Us you will get an amazing experience and sure you will enjoy it. Come lets join Us...

Features of PEAK

  • simple and user friendly.
  • make a good visualized video and download it your system.
  • make a playlist for your favuorite music.

How to use PEAK:

  • Create a user profile using Sign Up option, if you are first time in PEAK.
  • Log in into your profile by applying credentials.
  • Add your favourite music from your system and save it.
  • Click Play option to enjoy the amazing visualizing of your music..
  • Click Download option to save the music with visualizing effect to your *system as video format.
